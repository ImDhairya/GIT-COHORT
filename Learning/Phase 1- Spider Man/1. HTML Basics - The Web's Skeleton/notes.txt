<section>
Represents a thematic grouping of content.
Often used to divide content into sections, like chapters in a document or parts of a webpage.
When to Use:

When content is related to a single theme.
To group content under a heading.


<article>
Represents a self-contained piece of content that can stand alone.
Often used for blog posts, news articles, or user-generated content.
When to Use:

When content can be independently distributed or reused.


<aside>
Represents content related to the main content, such as sidebars, pull quotes, or advertisements.
Often used for complementary information.
When to Use:

For tangential or supplementary content.
